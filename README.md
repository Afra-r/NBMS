<h1>Key Releases Suggestions:</h1>



* Funeral Mist's Devilry EP from 1998 is considered one of their most important releases, I think it is worth adding as a key release
* Thorns - Trøndertun is another one I would personally consider a key release
* Abruptum - Evil Genius is just a compilation of older demos, I wouldn't consider it a key release
* Mysticum - Medusa's Tears also Industries of Inferno I wouldn't consider key releases, especially since the latter is just a boxset of old demos
* Gaahls Wyrd - Braiding the Stories is their latest LP, I would personally consider it a key release
